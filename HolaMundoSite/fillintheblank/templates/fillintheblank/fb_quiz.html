Instructions

There are a few ways you are able to create a Fill In The Blank Question.

  • Step 1: Fill out the "Start of Question" for the beginning of your sentence.
  • Step 2: Fill out the "Blank" for the word that the Student will fill out.
  • Step 3: Then fill out "End of Question" for the end of the sentence.
    • Step 4: You may also fill out the "Start of Question" and the "Blank" for your question. Example: The color of the sky is blue
  • Step 5: You may also start a sentence with "Blank" but you must fill out "End of Question". Example: George Washington was the 1st President of the United States.
